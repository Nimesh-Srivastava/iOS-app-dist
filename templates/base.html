App Sharing Feature
How App Sharing Works

As an admin, you have access to all apps in the system. Regular users can only see apps that have been shared with them.

Admin View
  • See all apps in the system
  • Upload and manage all apps
  • Control which users can access each app
  • Share apps with specific users
User View
  • See only apps shared with them
  • Download and install shared apps
  • Cannot see apps not shared with them
  • Cannot share apps with others
How to Share Apps

You can share apps with users in two ways:

  1. From an app's detail page, use the quick sharing form in the sidebar
  2. Click the "Manage Sharing" button to see advanced sharing options
Tip: Create regular user accounts for your team members or clients to control their access to specific apps.
